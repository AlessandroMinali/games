SEQUENCE OF PLAY
1. 'Make Ready' a Toff/Snob who hasn't acted this round.
2. Choose unit in range to order. Cannot Order other Snobs.
3. Declare Order for unit.
4. Check for Blunder.
5. Execute Order.
Keep alternating turns. If one player runs out of Snobs the other player may keep acting until all their Snobs have been Made Ready.
Once all Snobs have gone then each follower who hasn't be given an order repeats 3-4, but now may suffer Scenario Blunders.

RETREATING
1. Determine furthest model from the enemy and move that model directly away from the enemy no less than d6 + 2" per panic token.
2. Keep moving backwards if you cannot find a space which obeys the 1" RULE.
3. Make a Dangerous Terrain Test each time you cross over any Follower or move through Dangerous Terrain.

TARGETING: Must target closest enemy within line of sight(LoS). All units have 360deg field of view.
IMPROBABLE HIT(ie. 7+): Roll an unmodified 6 then roll again unmodified 4+ to hit.
LINE OF SIGHT(LoS): Blocked by all units(except Snobs) and terrain if it obscures almost all of a model. If one model has LoS, then the entire unit does as well.
RANGE: Any part of target model must be within the specified value. If one model is in range, then the entire unit is as well.

1" RULE
1. Must end all movement 1" away from friendly units unless friendly Snob or charging target enemy.
2. Must never cross within 1" of enemy unless charging or retreating.
3. May only cross through other units while retreating.

BLUNDER TEST: d6, on a 1 then unit receives a panic token and follow blunder penalty. Snobs can never blunder.
SCENARIO BLUNDER: Instead of normal blunder, refer to the scenario for what happens.
PANIC TEST: d6 + panic tokens on unit, if greater than 6 then the unit retreats.

DANGEROUS TERRAIN TEST
1. roll d6 per model in the unit
2. Unit must remove models equal to the 1s rolled.
3. Unit gains 1 panic token if any models were removed.

TERRAIN
Cover(6"x1"): If target has half their models behind and within 3" of cover then +1 Inaccuracy to shooter. 
Defensible(6"x6"): If within then target is Fearless, if wholly within then also +1 Inaccuracy to shooter.
Impassable(6"x6"): On contact, unit must remove models equal to units panic tokens. Includes board edge.
Dangerous(9"x9"): Causes a Dangerous Terrain Test when actively moved through.

INITIATIVE: Each player rolls d6 until one rolls higher, they go first.
REGIMENT: 1 Toff + 2 Followers, 2 Snobs with 1 Follower each. Equip all units.
DEPLOYMENT: Snob + attached Followers within deployment area alternating between players.
ROUND: Once all units have performed an order or been Made Ready.
GAME: 4 rounds.

ORDERS
1. Volley Fire!
  a. Reduce Inaccuracy by 1 unless Blunder.
  b. Apply any other modifiers for shooting attacks.
  c. Enter a Shooting Engagement with target in LoS.
2. Move and Shoot!
  a. Move up to your full Movement or just d6" if Blunder, commanding Snob may follow.
  b. Roll any Dangerous Terrain Tests.
  c. Apply any modifiers for shooting attacks.
  d. Enter a Shooting Engagement with closest target in LoS.
3. March!
  a. Move up to full Movement + 2d6" (or Movement + d6" if Blunder), commanding Snob may follow.
  b. Roll any Dangerous Terrain Tests.
4. Charge!
  a. Choose closest target within LoS and within Movement + 12" (or Movement + 6" if Blunder).
  b. Target makes a Panic Test.
  c. If Panicked enemy, then step e.
  d. Target must Stand and Shoot if able. Target does not need to be range or have LoS to shoot. Target enters a Shooting Engagement but charging unit cannot fire back. If charging unit is not wiped out proceed to the next step.
  e. Move towards the target Movement + 2d6" (or Movement + d6" if Blunder) by the shortest route possible, commanding Snob may follow.
  f. Roll any Dangerous Terrain Tests.
  g. If any charging model makes base to base contact with target then both units fight a Melee.

SHOOTING ENGAGEMENT
If they enemy unit can it must Return Fire.
1. Each valid side rolls d6 per model in their unit and scores a "hit" for each die greater or equal than their own Inaccuracy.
2. Each valid side rolls d6 per "hit" received from the enemy and receives a Wound to their unit for each die below its Vulnerability.
3. Grant Wounds, removing models that reach their Wound limit and allocating remaining Wound to other models remaining in the unit.
4. Determine winner by who ever inflicted the most Wound. Loser, if any, retreats.
5. Units that were "hit", even if no Wound occurred, gain a panic token.

MELEE
Charging player starts first.
1. Rolls d6 equal to combined Attack of every model of the charging unit and score a "hit" for each die greater or equal than their own Inaccuracy.
2. Opponent rolls d6 per "hit" received from the enemy and receives a Wound to their unit for each die below their own Vulnerability.
3. Grant Wounds, removing models that reach their Wound value and allocating remaining Wound to other models remaining in the unit.
4. The opposing side repeats step 1-3 then go step 5.
5. Determine winner by who ever inflicted the most Wound in this Melee. Loser retreats. If draw then start a new Melee. Automatically declared winner if opposing side is destroyed.
6. Both units gain a panic token.


EQUIPMENT
Follower Equipment
  a. Black Powder Weapons(Ranged): Can only shoot once per round.
  b. Missile Weapons(Ranged): Target -2 Vulnerability.
  c. Close Combat Weapons: -1 Inaccuracy on melee attacks. May re-roll charge distance, keep new result.
Snob Equipment
  a. Pistols and Sabres(Ranged): -


STATS
     Movement (M) Maximum distance unit may move in a round.
       Attack (A) Number of dice rolled per model in a Melee.
   Inaccuracy (I) Minimum result needed to land an attack.
Vulnerability (V) Minimum result needed to avoid receiving a wound.
        Wound (W) Number of wounds a model may suffer before being slain.
